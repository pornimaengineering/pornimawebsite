About us

Welcome to Pornima Engineering Industries, your trusted partner in solar energy solutions. As a leading Solar Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) company, we are dedicated to transforming the way the world uses energy. With a commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability, we deliver comprehensive solar energy systems tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Govt. approved Empanelled Vendor

We are empanelled with MNRE and MAHADISCOM under PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana.

What We Do

We offer a full range of services that cover every aspect of solar energy projects:

Engineering

Our team of skilled engineers designs customized solar energy systems that maximize efficiency and performance. Using the latest technology and industry best practices, we ensure our designs meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Procurement

We source the best solar components from leading manufacturers worldwide. Our strong relationships with suppliers guarantee that we procure high-quality materials at competitive prices, ensuring the best value for our clients.

Construction

Our experienced construction teams handle the installation of solar systems with precision and care. From residential rooftops to large-scale commercial projects, we manage every aspect of construction to ensure timely and seamless project completion.
