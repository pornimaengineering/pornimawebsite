About Us

 

Who We Are

Welcome to Pornima Engineering Industries, your trusted partner in solar energy solutions. As a leading Solar Engineering, Procurement, and Construction (EPC) company, we are dedicated to transforming the way the world uses energy. With a commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability, we deliver comprehensive solar energy systems tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

What We Do

We offer a full range of services that cover every aspect of solar energy projects:

Engineering

Our team of skilled engineers designs customized solar energy systems that maximize efficiency and performance. Using the latest technology and industry best practices, we ensure our designs meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Procurement

We source the best solar components from leading manufacturers worldwide. Our strong relationships with suppliers guarantee that we procure high-quality materials at competitive prices, ensuring the best value for our clients.

Construction

Our experienced construction teams handle the installation of solar systems with precision and care. From residential rooftops to large-scale commercial projects, we manage every aspect of construction to ensure timely and seamless project completion.

Our Mission

At Pornima Engineering Industries, Our mission is to harness the power of the sun to create a brighter, more sustainable future for generations to come. Through innovative solar solutions, we aim to empower individuals, businesses, and communities to embrace renewable energy, reduce their carbon footprint, and build a world where clean energy is accessible to all. We are committed to delivering exceptional quality, reliability, and service, driving positive change one solar panel at a time.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a driving force in the transition towards a renewable energy future, where solar power is not only the norm but the cornerstone of sustainable living worldwide. We envision a world where every roof is a power plant, every community generates its own clean energy, and every individual has the opportunity to contribute to a greener planet. Through our dedication to innovation, excellence, and community engagement, we strive to lead the charge towards a brighter, cleaner, and more prosperous tomorrow powered by the sun.

Our Values

Innovation
We are committed to staying at the forefront of the solar industry through continuous innovation. By embracing new technologies and creative solutions, we enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of our solar energy systems.

Quality
Quality is at the heart of everything we do. From the materials we select to the systems we install, we maintain rigorous standards to ensure long-lasting performance and customer satisfaction.

Sustainability
We believe in the power of renewable energy to create a sustainable future. Our projects are designed to minimize environmental impact and promote the use of clean, renewable energy sources.

Integrity
We conduct our business with the highest levels of integrity and transparency. Building trust with our clients, partners, and communities is essential to our success.
